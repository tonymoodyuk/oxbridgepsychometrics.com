Case Studies

Selected examples illustrating how Oxbridge Psychometrics has supported pharmaceutical programmes through rigorous psychometric methodology and regulator-aligned evidence development.

Development of a Patient-Reported Outcome Measure

Context

A pharmaceutical sponsor required a new patient-reported outcome instrument to capture treatment benefits not fully reflected in clinical endpoints.

Our Contribution

Development of a fit-for-purpose questionnaire including conceptual framework development, item generation, and psychometric evaluation aligned with FDA PRO guidance.

Advantage Delivered

Strong evidence of content validity, reliability, and sensitivity to change, supporting regulatory-facing analyses.

Psychometric Evaluation to Strengthen Regulatory Evidence

Context

Existing outcome data required additional psychometric justification to meet regulatory expectations for interpretation and use.

Our Contribution

Detailed evaluation of measurement properties including construct validity, internal consistency, and responsiveness.

Advantage Delivered

Clear, regulator-ready justification supporting the credibility of outcome measures within the overall evidence package.

Optimisation of Outcome Strategy Across a Development Programme

Context

A development programme required alignment of outcome measurement strategy across multiple studies and timepoints.

Our Contribution

Strategic input on outcome selection, measurement timing, and psychometric evidence requirements to ensure consistency and regulatory relevance.

Advantage Delivered

A coherent, defensible outcome strategy supporting interpretation of patient benefit across the programme lifecycle.
