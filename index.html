What We Do

Demonstrating meaningful patient benefit requires more than clinical efficacy. We help pharmaceutical organisations capture, quantify, and validate patient experiences using scientifically robust psychometric approaches that stand up to regulatory scrutiny.

Our Services

Questionnaire Development

Design and development of fit-for-purpose questionnaires to capture expected patient impacts, grounded in qualitative research and psychometric best practice.

Psychometric Analysis

Statistical evaluation of reliability, validity, sensitivity, and responsiveness to demonstrate that instruments accurately measure patient-relevant outcomes.

Regulatory-Ready Evidence

Analysis and documentation aligned with regulatory standards, including FDA guidance, to support submissions, labeling discussions, and value demonstration.

Why Work With Oxbridge Psychometrics

Our work sits at the intersection of psychometrics, patient-centred research, and regulatory science. We understand both the methodological rigor required and the practical realities of regulatory review.

The result is evidence that is scientifically defensible, clearly communicated, and appropriate for decision-making by regulatory authorities.

Prof Georgina Jones and Dr Anthony Moody are highly experienced psychometricians who have come together in this recently established business. Both completed their doctorates at University of Oxford over 20 years ago and have worked independently as psychometricians. Georgina is the Professor of Health Psychology and Director of the Centre for Patient-Reported Outcomes at the Leeds Beckett University, UK. Anthony was Lead Psychometrician of UK Central Government in the Cabinet Office.
